Crypto Safety Internship App

I volunteered as an intern at TheCryptoPlayground and was then offered full-time work. I single handedly created a working demo app for the company to show investors using React and Canva.
The app aims to educate the public about crypto safety, by simulating crypto transactions and showing areas of risk. I connected the appto CoinGecko API so it had a live simulated crypto exchange.
