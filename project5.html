Euros Prediction Model

I am a big football fan and for every major tournament I host a sweepstake score predictor with a group of friends.
Every participant predicts the score of every match in an excel spreadsheet and my code generates automatic updates on their positions based on the accuracy of their guesses (See Above). By using web scraping and conneting to the Whatsapp API my code sends live score and points updates throughout the tournament on a groupchat.
But this year I also created a prediction model by analyzing performances of previous teams and past scorelines. Using scoring trends and odds I created a Euro 2024 tournament predictor.
And I achieved a third place finish out of 50 participants.
