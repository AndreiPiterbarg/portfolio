Perfecting a Wordle Strategy Using Machine Learning

As an independant research project I wanted to explore key features in ML.
I started with a simple Bayesian model before moving onto making my own Unsupervised model and Autoencoder from scratch.
My models, simulates wordle attempts with different strategies and starting words to find the optimum one. My best model could complete the wordle in an average of 3.6 guesses, with the average human doing it in 4.
